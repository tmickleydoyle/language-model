How to make homemade lemonade:
1. Squeeze fresh lemons to get one cup of juice.
2. In a pitcher, combine the lemon juice with one cup of sugar.
3. Add five cups of cold water and stir until the sugar dissolves.
4. Add ice cubes and lemon slices for garnish.
5. Taste and adjust sweetness as needed.
6. Serve chilled and enjoy on a hot day.
