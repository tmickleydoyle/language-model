How to make a simple salad:
1. Wash and chop lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumbers.
2. Place the vegetables in a large bowl.
3. Add sliced carrots, bell peppers, or other favorite veggies.
4. Toss with olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
5. Add cheese, nuts, or croutons if desired.
6. Serve immediately as a side or main dish.
