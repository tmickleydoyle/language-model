How to plant a vegetable garden:
1. Choose a sunny location with good soil.
2. Clear the area of weeds and debris.
3. Dig the soil and mix in compost.
4. Plan your garden layout, leaving space between rows.
5. Plant seeds or seedlings according to package instructions.
6. Water the garden regularly, keeping the soil moist.
7. Remove weeds as they appear.
8. Harvest vegetables when they are ripe and enjoy fresh produce.
