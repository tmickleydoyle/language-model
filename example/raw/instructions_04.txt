How to change a flat tire:
1. Park your car on a flat, stable surface and turn on the hazard lights.
2. Apply the parking brake and place wheel wedges behind the tires.
3. Remove the hubcap and loosen the lug nuts with a wrench.
4. Use a jack to lift the car off the ground.
5. Remove the lug nuts and take off the flat tire.
6. Mount the spare tire onto the wheel studs.
7. Tighten the lug nuts by hand, lower the car, then fully tighten the nuts with the wrench.
8. Replace the hubcap and store the tools.
