The internet has revolutionized the way people communicate, work, and access information. In the early days, only a few computers were connected through simple networks. Today, billions of devices are linked worldwide, enabling instant communication and the sharing of vast amounts of data. Social media platforms allow users to connect with friends and family, while online marketplaces have transformed shopping. The rise of cloud computing has made it possible to store and process data remotely, increasing efficiency and collaboration. As technology continues to evolve, cybersecurity and privacy remain important concerns for individuals and organizations alike.
