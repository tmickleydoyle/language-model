How to write a formal email:
1. Start with a clear subject line.
2. Use a polite greeting, such as 'Dear Mr. Smith.'
3. State your purpose in the opening paragraph.
4. Provide necessary details in the body of the email.
5. Use proper grammar and punctuation.
6. End with a courteous closing, such as 'Sincerely.'
7. Proofread your email before sending.
