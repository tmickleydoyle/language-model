How to make a fruit smoothie:
1. Gather your favorite fruits, such as bananas, berries, and mango.
2. Wash and peel the fruits as needed.
3. Place the fruit in a blender.
4. Add a cup of yogurt or milk for creaminess.
5. Add a handful of ice cubes.
6. Blend until smooth.
7. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.
