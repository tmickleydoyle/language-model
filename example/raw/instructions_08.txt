How to start a home compost bin:
1. Choose a bin or container with good ventilation.
2. Place it in a convenient outdoor location.
3. Add a layer of dry leaves or shredded paper.
4. Add kitchen scraps like fruit and vegetable peels.
5. Alternate layers of green and brown materials.
6. Turn the compost regularly to aerate it.
7. Keep the compost moist but not soggy.
8. In a few months, use the finished compost in your garden.
