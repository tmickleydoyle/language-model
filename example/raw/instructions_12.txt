How to make a paper airplane:
1. Start with a rectangular sheet of paper.
2. Fold the paper in half lengthwise and crease.
3. Unfold and fold the top corners to the center crease.
4. Fold the angled edges to the center again.
5. Fold the plane in half along the original crease.
6. Fold the wings down to create a flat surface.
7. Hold the plane at the bottom and launch it gently.
