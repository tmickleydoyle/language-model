How to set a table for dinner:
1. Place a plate in the center of each setting.
2. Set a fork to the left of the plate and a knife to the right.
3. Place a spoon to the right of the knife if needed.
4. Set a glass above the knife for water or other drinks.
5. Place a napkin to the left of the fork or on the plate.
6. Add any additional utensils or dishes as needed for the meal.
