How to care for a houseplant:
1. Place the plant in a spot with the right amount of sunlight.
2. Water the plant according to its needs, avoiding overwatering.
3. Use well-draining soil and a pot with drainage holes.
4. Fertilize the plant during the growing season.
5. Remove dead leaves and check for pests regularly.
6. Repot the plant if it outgrows its container.
